The New Paradigm: Agentic Application Development

Welcome to a pivotal moment in technology. We are witnessing a fundamental shift where the cost of generating software is plummeting by a factor of 10-100x. This isn't just an incremental improvement; it's a paradigm shift that challenges the dominance of traditional SaaS models. The era of bespoke, AI-generated applications is here, and it's making custom software development faster, cheaper, and more accessible than ever before.

This transformation democratizes creation. The ability to build software is no longer confined to developers. Product owners, business analysts, and designers can now directly translate their vision into functional applications, shipping features and iterating on ideas at unprecedented speeds. This is the core of Agentic Application Development.

However, this power comes with significant challenges. To move from a clever prototype to a secure, scalable, and compliant enterprise application requires a new set of skills and frameworks. How do we ensure security? How do we manage governance and IP? How do we build systems that are maintainable? Tackling these challenges is the central goal of this hackathon. We are here to build the serious, structured capabilities needed to deliver real client value in this new world.

Your Mission: From Idea to Enterprise-Grade Prototype

Your challenge is to tackle real-world business problems and build solutions that are not just functional, but demonstrate a clear path to production. This requires a business-first framing and a commitment to enterprise-grade implementation.

  • Goal: Develop an end-to-end solution for a sanitized, real-world client scenario, proving the value of an AI-native SDLC.
  • Team: Form cross-functional teams that combine business acumen, industry expertise, and technical skill.
  • Approach: Think beyond the code. Your solution must consider security, governance, documentation, and scalability from the start.

The Ultimate Goal:

To build the skills and frameworks that will allow us to confidently approach clients with a new, agentic way of delivering value, positioning our network as pioneers in this space.

Reinvention Themes: Disruptive Enterprise Scenarios

This hackathon is about bold ideas. When you can develop applications 100x faster, entirely new ways of solving problems emerge. Your challenge is to explore these disruptive scenarios.

Theme 1: Legacy Modernization as Reinvention

The Disruption: Build, Don't Just Lift-and-Shift

A client is trapped by a costly, inflexible legacy system. The traditional approach is a multi-year migration project to a similar, modern system. The AI-native approach is different: generate a brand-new, perfectly tailored application from business rules and user stories in a fraction of the time and cost.

Your Mission: Prototype a core module of a legacy system (e.g., CRM, ERP) as a new, AI-generated application. Demonstrate how you can go from business requirements to a functional, secure, and scalable application, leapfrogging the traditional modernization lifecycle.

Theme 2: SaaS Re-platforming & The End of "Good Enough"

The Disruption: Why Buy When You Can Build?

Clients often compromise with off-the-shelf SaaS products that are 80% right but 100% rigid. When development is 100x cheaper, building a bespoke solution that is 100% right becomes the smarter financial and operational choice. The "build vs. buy" calculation has fundamentally changed.

Your Mission: Identify a common SaaS category (e.g., project management, HR onboarding, expense reporting) and build a superior, AI-generated alternative. Show how your custom-built solution offers better workflow integration, data control, and a lower total cost of ownership than a standard SaaS subscription.

Theme 3: Business Process Reinvention

The Disruption: From Manual Mess to Agentic Automation

Many core business processes still run on a chaotic mix of emails, spreadsheets, and manual data entry. These processes are too niche or complex for standard automation tools. Agentic Application Development allows us to build hyper-specific tools that automate these unique workflows instantly.

Your Mission: Take a known manual business process (e.g., claims processing, customer onboarding, report generation) and build an AI-powered application that completely automates it. Your solution should demonstrate how an agentic workflow can replace hours of manual work, reduce errors, and provide real-time insights.

The Agentic Development Toolkit

We believe in freedom of tooling. While we provide a set of recommended and supported tools, you are encouraged to choose your own tools of the trade. The focus is on building enterprise-grade, server-based applications that run on containers or VMs with databases, accessible on the web.

Choose Your Orchestration Path

1. Human Orchestration with Vibecoding

Use pre-built user interfaces from vibecoding systems (e.g., Lovable, Firebase Studio) to prompt what you want. This path is great for understanding the end-to-end flow of work and identifying gaps in current systems.

Focus: Business problem and rapid prototyping.

2. Partial Automation (Integrated CI/CD)

Combine human expertise with AI. Use AI agents within your IDE (like Cursor or Copilot) to generate artifacts, and a classic CI/CD pipeline (like GitHub Actions or Azure DevOps) to deploy.

Focus: Blending AI assistance with traditional DevOps.

3. End-to-End Agentic Orchestration

Build an agentic AI layer on top of different components. Use an orchestration engine (like LangChain or AutoGen) to manage a pipeline of AI tools that generates, builds, tests, and deploys your application from a single prompt.

Focus: Showcasing a true "Idea to App" automated flow.
